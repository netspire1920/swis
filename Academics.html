Eligibility & Documents Required

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for Admission to FYJC Science / Commerce:

A student who has successfully completed the SSC Examination conducted by the Divisional Board of Secondary Education in the State of Maharashtra is eligible for admission to FYJC (Science/Commerce).

OR

A student who has passed an examination from another board considered equivalent to the SSC Examination conducted by the Divisional Boards in the State of Maharashtra is eligible for admission to FYJC (Science/Commerce). In such cases where the candidate is not a student of any of the divisional Boards of Maharashtra State, they need to obtain an eligibility certificate from the Maharashtra State Secondary and Higher Secondary Board, Vashi, Navi Mumbai. At the time of admission, students must enclose the following documents along with the application form, as per the directive issued by the Director of Education, Government of Maharashtra.
Admission to FYJC is granted based on merit, and seats are reserved according to government policy.

List Of Required

Documents for Admission to FYJC Science / Commerce:

Students who have passed the SSC Examination from the Maharashtra State Board are required to submit the following documents at the time of admission:

  • Completed Admission Form.
  • Original Mark Sheet along with 2 attested photocopies.
  • Original School Leaving Certificate along with 2 attested photocopies.
